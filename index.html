About Me

Innovative Research in Catalysis & Plasma Technology

I am a passionate chemical engineer with extensive experience in catalysis research, specializing in nanostructured materials, heterogeneous catalysis, and non-thermal plasma applications for environmental remediation and energy production.

My research focuses on developing novel catalytic systems for VOC abatement, dry/steam reforming processes, and selective catalytic reduction of pollutants. I have expertise in designing and testing various reactor systems for environmental and energy applications.
